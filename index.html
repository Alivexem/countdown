How to use

Welcome to AlivexemTech Countdown. Input a number of your choice in the section for input countdown. Select your preferred countdown type and click the start button.

To stop countdown, click the pause button and you also have an option to resume countdown when you click the button again.

To refresh, click the refresh button.
